About the Website

University Event Hub is a centralized platform designed to keep students, faculty, and staff updated with all the upcoming events, activities, and schedules within the university. We display all schedules in one place. We also showcase the different food and game booths available during these events, making it easier for everyone to plan and enjoy.

Vision

To be the leading digital platform that connects the university community through efficient information dissemination, fostering engagement, and making every event accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Mission

To provide accurate, real-time updates on university events and booth locations, ensuring that no student or staff misses out on the excitement and opportunities within the campus.
